About Nermine

After 15 years analyzing and designing business strategies across startups, corporations, and venture capital firms, I now help business owners create financial strategies that serve both their life and business needs.

Only 16% of self-employed professionals make private pension contributions. I wasn't surprised reading that. Since founding Molequl, I've discovered that none of my clients had retirement plans in place. Their only savings came from previous full-time employment. The irony? Each started their business seeking greater flexibility and control over their future.

The root causes are primarily psychological. The combination of hustle culture and decision fatigue makes personal finance both harder to address and a lower priority. Paradoxically, this creates the opposite of control.

My mission is clear: build a holistic framework for business owners so their business truly serves them, not the other way around.
